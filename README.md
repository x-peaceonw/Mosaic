# Mosaic
What is this? Different pieces of things i love brought into one place. No noise. No ads. Just what i want to see. In the way i want to see it. And i am not the last to know anymore. All while Tracking my interest. 
Why does it exist? For fun. i love media. but social media is not good for my mental health.
